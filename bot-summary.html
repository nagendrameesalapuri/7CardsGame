Safe Bot

Beginner-friendly · Pure self-preservation
Bot Win Rate85 – 90%

Plays pure self-preservation. Draws from the deck almost always, avoids risky cuts, and calls SHOW the moment its hand total hits ≤5. It never bluffs, never baits, and throws 7s immediately without holding them for pressure. Ideal as the Stage 1 opponent — loses often enough to be a confidence builder.

Bluff Bot

Most deceptive · Creates real uncertainty
Bot Win Rate50 – 60%

The most deceptive bot. Has a 20% chance each turn to execute a "bluff tactical line" — deliberately discards one card from a medium-value pair (5–8 pts) to look like it's clearing, while keeping the partner. Uses fake hesitation spikes (+600–1400ms extra delay 18% of the time) to simulate deep thinking. Shows at ≤9 pts — sometimes winning, sometimes failing — making it genuinely hard to read.

Smart Bot

Full strategist · Reads your game
Bot Win Rate35 – 45%

A full strategic opponent. Runs 2-turn lookahead before every discard, scores the opponent benefit of each card it considers throwing (jokers=4.0, Aces=2.8, 2s=2.2 — avoids giving you what you need), and tracks your behavior over time to identify your archetype. Its 4% controlled imperfection keeps it human-feeling and unsolvable.
